Rachael Burke

Comedian · New York, NY · rachaelburkecomedy@gmail.com

Rachael Burke is a two-time Emmy Award winning comedian based in New York City. Rachael spent over 5 years at The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon and is experienced in writing and performing comedy in a variety of mediums.

Individual Work

Rachael has contributed countless on-air jokes and sketches to the Tonight Show, including this sketch about Ariana Grande’s ponytail coming alive starring Ariana Grande and Jimmy Fallon that garnered over 13 million views across YouTube and Facebook.

Rachael also writes and performs unique and original stand-up, improv, sketch comedy, characters, and a broad array of impressions including Ariana Grande, Senator Elizabeth Warren, Adam Sandler, Melania Trump, Lorde, and Julie Andrews among others. Rachael sketch featuring her Elizabeth Warren impression was featured on Elizabeth Banks’ comedy website WhoHaha, and was ranked #1 on that website for weeks.

Group Work

In 2018, Rachael founded a sketch group called Kids These Days, made up of producers and writers from several late night comedy shows including The Daily Show, Full Frontal with Samantha Bee, and The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon. Rachael single-handedly scouted and recruited each of the writers and performers to the group. She produces the shows in addition to acting and writing for the group. Kids These Days has a recurring monthly mainstage show at the People's Improv Theater and has performed at the San Francisco SketchFest, New York Comedy Fest, Charleston Comedy Festival, Out of Bounds Comedy Festival, New York SketchFest and the HBO and Television Academy’s Independent Television Festival.
